Dead Prom Queen

Difficulty Rating: 7/10

I did this look for a Halloween party that was themed “Haunted Wedding.” Obviously, I decided to go as a dead prom queen.

  1. Apply your base. This includes foundation, concealer and setting powder. Feel free to use shades a little lighter than your normal skin tone to increase that "dead" look
  2. Contour using a dark grey powder/eyeshadow. Using grey to contonur your cheeks and forehead instead of a deeper skin tone color will add to the "dead" appearance.
  3. Use a dark purple eyeshadow and apply generously under your eyes. The purple is used to simulate the appearance of bruises and dark circles. Blend the purple shadow anywhere you want the appearance of bruises.
  4. Do your eyebrows as your normally would. I used dip brow pomade for this step.
  5. Set your eyelids with eyeshadow primer to make the color really pop. I kept this eye look simple using purple and dark grey to create a deep smokey eye. Finally, I added a little bit of highlight on my inner corner.
  6. Complete the look with a dark lipstick and false lashes
  7. Take the look to the next step by dripping fake blood on your face/chest. Feel free to bring the grey contour and purple eyeshadow down your neck/chest to bring it to the next level!
